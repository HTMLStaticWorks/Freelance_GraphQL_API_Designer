Before You Reach Out

Quick Answers

What information should I include in my message?

The more context the better: your current stack (Apollo Server, Yoga, etc.), the main problem you're facing, approximate number of types in your schema, and your timeline. Screenshots of error messages or resolver code are welcome too.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, absolutely. I'm happy to sign your company's NDA before any technical discussions. Just include it in your first email.

How do payments work?

I invoice via Stripe or wire transfer. Projects are typically 50% upfront, 50% on completion. For retainer work, monthly invoicing is available.
