My Story

From REST to GraphQL Obsession

I started building APIs in 2018 with REST, hitting walls of over-fetching, versioning nightmares, and client-specific endpoints. When I discovered GraphQL, the elegance was immediate — but so were the traps.

After spending two years fixing N+1 problems, schema anti-patterns, and resolver bottlenecks for teams that had adopted GraphQL without guardrails, I realized there was a clear gap: most teams needed someone who understood both the philosophy and the performance engineering.

GQLForge is that bridge. I help teams design schemas they're proud of, resolve performance issues they've been fighting for months, and build the federation architecture that lets them scale independently.
